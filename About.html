ABOUT

History:

SewerSlvt (aka. as Jvne, previously Jvnko) is an Australian based music producer. They specialise in electronic music, specifically the breakcore genre with darker themes. Their first album 'Drowning in the sewer' was released in July of 2019 containing 9 songs. Throughout Jvne's works many references can be spotted such as notable anime characters on album covers, however, one of the most noteable references being their 'Cyberia Lyr1+2=3' album. The cover and name of the album and songs are a reference to the anime 'Serial Experiments Lain', Cyberia being one of the locations in the anime. In addition, the songs also contain voice samples from some of the episodes.

Current:

Jvne's latest album, which is also their last, 'we had good times together don't forget that' was produced in memory of Jvne's late partner Angel who they lost due to suicide. On the last song of the youtube album titled 'goodbye' the music displays a series of google street-view locations which were of importance to the couple, such as where their first date was. At the end of the album we see a visual: 'Dedicated to the memory of Angel'. As of now Jvne no longer has an internet presence and their discord, patreon and merch store have all closed down. Once the merch store was sold out a large sum of the portions were donated to the Beyond Blue foundation, which aims to provice support for those struggling with mental health.
